Kafka Hibino(Kaiju No. 8)
Fighter/Marksman
Kafka Hibino is the main protagonist, an earnest young man working in the Defense Force's Kaiju Extermination Division. His ordinary life takes a dramatic turn when he becomes infected by the blood of a giant monster during a mission, granting him extraordinary powers and transforming him into the humanoid Kaiju No. 8. Kafka must grapple with his newfound abilities, navigate the complexities of friendship and duty, and confront the threats posed by both monstrous creatures and human adversaries. With his determination and sense of justice, Kafka emerges as a compelling and relatable hero in this action-packed manga series.
Mina Ashiro
Marksman
Mina Ashiro is a significant character and childhood friend of the protagonist, Kafka Hibino. Mina serves in the Defense Force alongside Kafka, and she holds a special place in his heart as his crush. Mina is depicted as strong-willed, capable, and dedicated to her duties within the organization. As the story unfolds, Mina plays a crucial role in Kafka's journey, offering support and friendship as he grapples with his newfound abilities as Kaiju No. 8. Her character adds depth and emotion to the narrative, contributing to the dynamic relationships within the series.
Leno Ichikawa
Marksman
Leno is a charismatic and ambitious character who serves as the Deputy Chief of the Defense Corps. He's known for his sharp intellect and strategic prowess, often taking charge of challenging situations with confidence. Despite his authoritative demeanor, Leno harbors a compassionate side, genuinely caring for his comrades and the safety of humanity against the kaiju threat. His unwavering dedication to duty and his ability to inspire others make him a pivotal figure in the ongoing battle against the monstrous creatures.
Soshiro Hoshina
Swordsman
Hoshina is a skilled and determined member of the Defense Corps. As a close friend and former classmate of the protagonist, Kafka Hibino, Hoshina shares a deep bond with him. He is characterized by his unwavering loyalty, often standing by Kafka's side through thick and thin. Hoshina's strength lies in his unwavering resolve and his willingness to put himself in harm's way to protect his friends and humanity from the kaiju threat. Despite facing numerous challenges, Hoshina remains a steadfast ally, embodying the spirit of camaraderie and sacrifice in the face of adversity.
Kikoru Shinomiya
Marksman
Kikoru Shinomiya is a determined and ambitious member of the Defense Corps. With a background in science and research, she brings a unique perspective to the team, often analyzing kaiju behavior and developing innovative strategies for combat. Kikoru is characterized by her strong sense of duty and her unwavering dedication to protecting humanity from the kaiju threat. Despite her initially reserved nature, she forms meaningful connections with her comrades, particularly Kafka Hibino, the protagonist. Kikoru's intelligence, resourcefulness, and empathy make her a valuable asset in the ongoing battle against the monstrous creatures.
Kaiju No. 9
Fighter/Mage
Kaiju No. 9 is a formidable and mysterious entity, representing a significant threat to humanity. Little is known about its origins or motivations, but it possesses immense power and intelligence. Kaiju No. 9's appearance heralds chaos and destruction, as it unleashes devastating attacks on cities and military forces. Its abilities surpass those of ordinary kaiju, posing a unique challenge to the Defense Corps. As the primary antagonist of the series, Kaiju No. 9 serves as a constant reminder of the dangers posed by the monstrous creatures and the urgent need to find a way to defeat them.
